## ğŸ“Š American Odds Explained

The Overseer uses American odds (standard in sports betting):

### Positive Odds (Underdog)
- **+150** means: Bet 100, win 150 profit (250 total)
- **+200** means: Bet 100, win 200 profit (300 total)
- Higher number = less likely to win, bigger payout

### Negative Odds (Favorite)
- **-110** means: Bet 110, win 100 profit (210 total)
- **-200** means: Bet 200, win 100 profit (300 total)
- Higher number = more likely to win, smaller payout
